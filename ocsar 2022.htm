Oscare 2022

The 2022 Academy Awards had a little of everything: comedy, drama, action – and
that was during the live portion of the ceremony itself. It was a welcome return to a
hosted event, the three and a half hours didn’t feel like three and a half hours, and,
overall, it was one of the best Oscar telecasts in some time. There were a few quirks:
playing ‘Africa’ by Toto when Daniel Kaluuya and H.E.R. came out to present was
an odd choice, to put it politely. However, as a celebration of film and the casts and
crews of the year’s nominations, it succeeded, with acceptance speeches that were
inspirational, joyful, and simply great. Here’s the best of the night.
